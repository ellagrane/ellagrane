Ella's Breakfast Scrambled

Recipe:

  1. Crack three eggs into a bowl
  2. Add a tbsp of almond milk to the eggs and mix
  3. Add the mixture onto a hot pan sprayed with avocado oil
  4. Add a pinch of shredded cheese and mix until cooked and fluffy
  5. Toast a slice of the Sourdough Boule from Trader Joes until golden brown
  6. Put the eggs and toast on a plate and spread butter along the toast
  7. Cover your eggs with Tabasco hot sauce
  8. Pour yourself a cup of Breakfast Blend Starbucks Coffee in a Keurig and you're done!

Ella's Eggs Benedict

Recipe:

  1. Toast an English muffin until golden brown
  2. Place two eggs into a pot of boiling water until cooked
  3. Purchase store-bought ham and hollandaise sauce
  4. Add a small slice of ham on top of each half of the English muffin
  5. Add the poached eggs on top of the ham
  6. Drizzle warm hollandaise sauce on top of the poached eggs
  7. Pour yourself a cup of Breakfast Blend Starbucks Coffee in a Keurig and you're done!
