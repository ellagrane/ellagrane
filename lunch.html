Ella's Burrata Salad

Recipe:

  1. Toast a slice of the Sourdough Boule from Trader Joe's until golden brown
  2. Drizzle olive oil and washed arugula on bread
  3. Chop up half of a burrata ball and place it on top of the arugula
  4. Top the salad off with a drizzle of balsamic vinegar glaze
  5. Refresh yourself with an apple cider vinegar infused Raspberry Rose sodaby Poppi and you're done!

Ella's Broccoli Macaroni and Cheese

Recipe:

  1. Boil a pot of hot water
  2. Add a box of Annie's White Cheddar Mac and Cheese once the water is boiled and leave for 10 minutes
  3. Boil another pot of hot water
  4. Place two handfuls of broccoli florets in the water for 4 minutes
  5. Strain the pasta and add a dash of almond milk and butter
  6. Add the cheese mixture that comes in the box to the pasta
  7. Smash the broccoli and add to the pasta and mix
  8. Refresh yourself with an apple cider vinegar infused Raspberry Rose soda by Poppi and you're done!
