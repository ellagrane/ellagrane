Ella's Grilled Cheese and Tomato Soup

Recipe:

  1. Purchase Tomato Feta Soup by Trader Joe's and add to a pot and cook until boiled
  2. Take two slices of the Sourdough Boule from Trader Joe's and butter all sides
  3. Take two slices of colby jack cheese and add to the bread
  4. Toast the sandwich in a panini press until golden brown
  5. Add the steaming soup to a bowl
  6. Once the sandwich is done, add to a plate and cut in half
  7. Refresh yourself with an apple cider vinegar infused Strawberry Lemon soda by Poppi and you're done!

Ella's Grilled Pesto Chicken Pasta

Recipe:

  1. Purchase Trader Joe's Raw Pesto Chicken Breast
  2. Add avocado oil to a pan and cook the chicken for five minutes on each side
  3. Boil a pot of water and add a dash of salt to the mix
  4. Once the water is boiling, add organic penne pasta and cook for 11 minutes
  5. Grate parmesan into a bowl while your pasta and chicken is cooking
  6. Once the chicken is done, chop it up into small pieces
  7. Once the pasta is cooked, strain it and add butter
  8. Add the chopped chicken, parmesan, and pasta into a bowl
  9. Refresh yourself with an apple cider vinegar infused Strawberry Lemon soda by Poppi and you're done!
